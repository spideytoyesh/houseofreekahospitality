Location

Perfectly connected
in Gurugram

Located near Sector 84, Gurugram, House of ReeKa sits at the intersection of comfort and connectivity. The neighbourhood includes a park, club, and sports facilities within the complex. Major NCR routes — Dwarka Expressway, NH-48, and the Southern Peripheral Road — are easily accessible. Metro stations, hospitals, and shopping malls are all a short drive away.


Whether you're visiting for a family holiday, a corporate trip, or a leisure getaway, the location ensures you're never far from where you need to be.
